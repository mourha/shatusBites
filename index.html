About the baker

Hello, I’m Aisha murtala, proud owner of shatus bites,My journey began after I graduated with no clear plans for the future. I decided to start small by baking pies at home. What began as a way to pass the time soon grew into a passion. Friends and family loved my pies, and their encouragement gave me the confidence to try new things. As time went on, I added more snacks like burgers and milk cakes to my menu. Slowly but surely, what started as a hobby turned into a business—Shatus Bites. Every bite we offer is made with care, using recipes I’ve perfected along the way.
